FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

  • What is Martini Market?

    Martini Market is a decentralized prediction market built on the XRP Ledger, enabling $MART token holders to create markets and speculate on the outcomes of real-world events such as politics, sports, asset prices, and more.

  • Which wallets can I use?

    You can use any wallet compatible with the XRP Ledger, such as Xaman or Ledger hardware wallets.

  • How are market outcomes determined?

    Markets are resolved using decentralized oracle solutions integrated directly into the XRP Ledger. This ensures accurate, transparent, and fair outcomes.

  • What is the $MART token?

    $MART is Martini Market's native utility token with a capped supply of 10 million. It is used for governance, market creation, staking rewards and fee incentives.

  • How can I buy MTM tokens?

    The $MART token isn't publicly available, however you can purchase $MART tokens during the public presale event or later directly on decentralized exchanges integrated with XRPL.
